Syntax

The syntax is composed of groups of one or two lines delimited by an empty line.

1. When there are two lines, the top row is for chords and bottom row for lyrics. When there are two lines the lines are aligned on matching spaces.

A pipe character (|) has a special meaning. When pipe characters align on all lines, a vertical bar is displayed in their place. 

                  | F                 | Em              | G        |
          This- _ | time I have real- | ly led my-self- | _ astray |

You can use broken pipe character (¦) to insert another type of break.

When the group has only one line, it is shown as is except when it's one of the following pragmas. 

          (no drums)

#song Song name
Set the title of the song.

#section Section name
Start a new section, for example verse or chorus. Lines of a section are kept together when possible. Section ends when another begins or at the end of text.

Use your browsers developer tools to tweak the style. For example in Firefox press shift-F7 to open the Style Editor. Or press right mouse button over outputted element, select Inspect Element then Style Editor.
